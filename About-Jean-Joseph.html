Database Expert, that believe in Database Design, Implementation and Performance Matter

Jean Joseph

Data Engineer

Acknowledged as a leader capable of mentoring teams of different backgrounds and talents and inspiring individual efforts to deliver. Organized and detail-oriented, with a positive pro-active and hardworking attitude.
