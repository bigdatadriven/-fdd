What are tech User Groups

Tech user groups are communities of technology enthusiasts who come together either on-premises or in virtual meetings to share knowledge, discuss trends, and collaborate. These groups focus on specific technologies, products, or geographic locations.

Members attend events, workshops, and presentations to learn, network, and stay updated on industry developments. Some user groups meet weekly, while others gather monthly. Meetings duration for 1-3 hour sessions featuring 1-2 guest speakers.

The Future Data-Driven Summit is an annual joint conference organized by multiple data platform user groups, serving as a gathering for enthusiasts in the field. About the Data Driven Community

Data Driven Community is a free organization with two branches: Cloud Data Driven User Group, focusing on data & AI, DevOps, data engineering & analytics, and machine learning; and Future Data Driven Summit, an online event showcasing the Microsoft Data Platform.
