About The Event

Future Data Driven is a marquis, online event focusing on the comprehensive Microsoft Data Platform. Our mission is to inform and update attendees with Data & AI, DevOps & DataOps, PowerBI & Visualization, Integration & Automation and cloud infrastructure. We are excited to welcome you to participate at our first ever Future Data Driven online event.
